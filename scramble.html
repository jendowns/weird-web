scrambled eggs recipe

*by my husband, David
  1. Add 2 tablespoons of butter in med high skillet
  2. Crack eggs into bowl, scramble
  3. Pour eggs into melted butter, don‚Äôt disturb butter layer for a minute or so.
  4. Eggs should be setting in a thin layer on the bottom. Using a wooden spoon start folding starting from the center of the pan out.
  5. Keep folding wet areas into the set areas. Rest for just a few seconds between folds, especially as it gets close to done.
  6. Eggs are done when there are no wet / shiny areas left. Not overcooking is key if you want the eggs spreadable on toast - remember they keep cooking even after the heat turns off.
